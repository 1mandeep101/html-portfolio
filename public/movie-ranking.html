Best Anime According To Mandeep Singh

My top 3 anime of all time.

Naruto

This is the best anime if anyone wants to get into anime,
This anime explores the ninja world and shows the struggle
of a kid named naruto who fights back from being a weak ninja
to eventually become the greatest ninja of all time

One Piece

This is another anime which explores the world of pirates
and exploring the sea and making friends along the way
who have their own ambition to achieve in their lives

Attack On Titan

This anime differs from the above mentioned , this show is
about titans who envade a secluded fortified walls in which
people live and they prey on humans and there are protecter
of the walls named scouts who fight back and discovers dark
secrets about the titans and their origins
